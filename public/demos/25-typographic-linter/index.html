Why We Built This

Typography has rules that everyone feels but nobody checks. Your eye catches the lone word dangling at the end of a paragraph — you just didn't have a name for it. We pointed pretext at the problem: instead of just flowing text, analyze the flow. Every orphan, every widow, every ragged edge, flagged in real time as you type or resize. A red pen for the shapes your words make.
