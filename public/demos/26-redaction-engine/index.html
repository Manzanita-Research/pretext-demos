Why We Built This

Redaction tools produce dead documents. Static PDFs with black bars pasted over text, broken layouts, ugly gaps where information used to be.

We wanted a document that stays alive at every classification level — reflowing in real time, always looking typeset, always readable. Pretext makes that possible at 60fps in a browser, with no DOM.

Every word you see on this page is rendered character-by-character onto a canvas. When redacted content reveals, the paragraph line-wrapping visibly changes. The layout engine recomputes the entire document on every frame.
