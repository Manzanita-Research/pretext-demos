Why We Built This

Reading is physical. Your eyes have preferences your conscious mind has never met. We built this because accessibility tools give you three presets and a reload — but comfort is a spectrum, not a menu. Drag a slider and watch the text reflow in real time until it feels right. The text here is real DOM — selectable, accessible, copy-pasteable. Pretext runs in the background measuring layout in microseconds, proving that JS-side text measurement is fast enough to power real-time UI without ever touching the DOM layout engine.
