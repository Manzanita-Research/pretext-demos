Why We Built This

Reading is physical. Your eyes have preferences your conscious mind has never met. We built this because accessibility tools give you three presets and a reload — but comfort is a spectrum, not a menu. Drag a slider and watch the text reflow in real time, at 60 frames per second, until it feels right. That's what a text engine should do.
