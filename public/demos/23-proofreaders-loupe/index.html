Why We Built This

Browser zoom is all-or-nothing. Pinch-to-zoom breaks layout. We wanted magnification that respects the reading experience — enlarge exactly what you need while the rest of the page stays coherent.

Under the loupe, text reflows at double size with comfortable spacing. Around it, every line recalculates its breaks in real time. Fifty lines, sixty times a second, zero jank.
