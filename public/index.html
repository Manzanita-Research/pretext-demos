Twenty-Eight Pretext Demos

We built these to explore pretext, Cheng Lou's text layout engine. Pure arithmetic, zero DOM reads, instant reflow. What happens when text becomes a material you can play like an instrument?

01

The Gutenberg Variations

Drag a slider from 1440 to 2026 and watch a single article reflow through 600 years of typographic history at 60fps.

Layout
 02

Concrete Poetry Machine

Type a sentence and it flows into the shape of its own meaning. "River" becomes sinuous, "cage" becomes bars, "heart" becomes a heart.

Interactive
 03

The Rosetta Engine

Latin, Arabic, and Chinese reflow simultaneously around a draggable stone. Three scripts solving the same spatial problem differently.

 04

Text Tectonics

Two paragraphs drift, collide, and subduct like tectonic plates. Mountains form at the collision zone. Typography gets geology.

Phonetic Topography

Audio reshapes the paragraph in real time. Frequency bands become line widths. Play a song and watch the text dance to it.

The Panopticon Reader

Text pools where you look and dissolves into letter-soup in your periphery. Your fovea is smaller than you think.

Interactive
 07

Babel Sieve

Words have mass proportional to their width. They fall through oscillating typographic slots. Short words drain like sand; long words dam up.

Cymatics Typograph

Audio drives Chladni nodal patterns. Text flows into the calm zones like sand on a vibrating plate, forming word-mandalas at 60fps.

The Flinch

A Treated Document

Babel Columns

Planetary Pulse

Climate data written into the shape of the text itself. Each line is one year. Wider lines mean a warmer planet.

Biofeedback typography. Your webcam detects motion and proximity. Hold still and the text calms. Move and it fractures.

Ouroboros

Text that describes its own layout. Resize the container and it rewrites itself to stay true. A fixed-point equation at 60fps.

The Shape Flow Editor

The Streaming Layout

AI chat streaming with zero layout shift. The pretext side predicts height before text renders. The DOM side janks. Side by side, the difference is devastating.

 23

The Proofreader's Loupe

The Adaptive Reader

Drag sliders for line width, height, and spacing — text reflows at 60fps until it feels right. Accessibility presets for dyslexia, low vision, and dense reading. Comfort is a spectrum, not a menu.

The Typographic Linter

Paste text and see every orphan, bad rag, and awkward break highlighted in real time. Resize the container and watch warnings appear and disappear.

The Redaction Engine

A government memo with a classification slider. Slide between Public and Top Secret — content reveals, redaction bars shrink, and the entire document reflows at 60fps.

 27

The Integral Page

After Karl Gerstner. A sea-voyage narrative whose typography is generated by its own content. Arguments get louder in type. Whispers shrink. Journalism becomes columns. The form is not designed — it is a programme.

The Perfect Paragraph

Type text and watch it find its own shape. Pretext tests 60 container widths in 0.04ms, scoring each for rag quality and orphans. The container adapts to the content, not the other way around.
